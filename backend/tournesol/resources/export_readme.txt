comparisons.csv:

This file contains all the public video comparisons made by Tournesol's users.


users.csv:

This file contains the list of the users who appear in comparisons.csv.

It also includes their trust score calculated from their email address (trusted or not) and the others users who
vouched for them. Users with a trust score of 0 (i.e. users without a trusted email and who haven't been vouched for
by trusted users) can influence together at most 10% of the total collaborative evaluation of a video.
